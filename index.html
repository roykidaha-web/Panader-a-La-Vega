A Taste of Tradition

Panadería La Vega is one of the last traditional bakeries in the area, beloved for generations. Our famous roquetes — crispy Canarian ring pastries — are made the old-fashioned way, lightly sweet and not too soft, just the way they've always been.

From the best bread on the lower island of Tenerife to fresh doughnuts and custom cakes, everything is crafted with care and delivered fresh every morning.
